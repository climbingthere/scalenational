Onboarding Checklist
Run through this regardless of tier. Don't skip Week 0 research — it's what makes the kick-off call worth something.
Week 0 Before Kick-off Call
  • Review client intake form
  • Research their brand: website, Instagram, current ads (check Ad Library), competitors
  • Identify 2–3 angles for first creative brief
  • Prepare package recommendation (if not already confirmed)
Kick-off Call Agenda
  • Confirm goals and KPIs — ROAS target, CPP target, monthly revenue goal
  • Get access: Meta Business Manager (they add ScaleNational as Partner), Instagram, website analytics
  • Confirm ad spend budget is separate from management fee
  • Agree on communication cadence
  • Contract signed + first invoice collected
Week 1 Discovery & Setup
  • Meta Pixel installed and firing correctly
  • Conversions API connected
  • UTM parameters set up
  • Facebook/Instagram pages linked to Ad Account
  • Business Manager access confirmed
  • Baseline audit: existing campaigns, audiences, pixel history
  • Brand voice doc created — tone, what to avoid, visual guidelines
  • Audience research doc — who buys, demographics, interests, competitor audiences
  • First creative brief written and sent to client for approval

Month 1 — Build & Test
Week 1
  • Complete onboarding checklist
  • Set up campaign structure: 1 TOF campaign, broad + 2 interest audiences
  • Launch with 2 static ads + 1 video creative
  • Set daily budget: client's ad spend ÷ 30
Week 2
  • Monitor daily: CTR, CPM, hook rate, landing page CVR
  • Cut anything with CTR below 0.8% after 3 days

  • Bi-weekly call: review early data, align on creative direction
Week 3

  • New UGC or AI video brief submitted based on winning angles

Week 4

  • Finalize month 1 report
  • Monthly report sent to client — spend, ROAS, CTR, CPP, top creative
Month 2 — Optimize
  • New creatives based on month 1 data
  • Scale winning ad sets by 20% budget increase
  • Test new audience: lookalike from pixel purchasers (if enough data)
  • Bi-weekly calls continue
Month 3 — Scale
  • Proven creative library established
  • Scale winning campaigns
  • Pitch renewal or upgrade to Growth package

Ongoing Operations
Daily, Weekly & Monthly
Run through these on a repeating basis. The daily check takes 15 minutes — don't skip it.
Daily ~15 min
  • Check all active campaigns: any ad account flags or rejections?
  • Check spend pacing — on track for monthly budget?
  • Any dramatic CPM spikes or drops? (Could indicate audience fatigue or algorithm shift)
  • Check any ads in review — approve or fix
Weekly ~1 hr per client
  • Pull weekly numbers: spend, ROAS, CTR, CPP, CPC
  • Compare to previous week — trending up or down?
  • Identify top 1–2 creatives by ROAS
  • Flag any ad sets running 2+ weeks with no conversions — kill or test new creative
  • Prep weekly call talking points
  • Update client dashboard
Bi-weekly / Monthly
  • Write new creative brief based on winning data
  • Source new UGC creator or generate AI video
  • Audience refresh — are current audiences fatiguing?
  • Competitor ad check — what's new in the Ad Library for their niche?
  • Monthly report: PDF or dashboard link sent to client
Signs to Watch For
Performance Red Flags
  • CTR drops below 0.8% on a previously winning ad → creative fatigue, refresh needed
  • CPM spikes 30%+ → audience saturation or increased competition — broaden or change audiences
  • ROAS drops 2 weeks in a row → escalate, review full funnel (ad → landing page → offer)
  • Ad account flags → fix immediately, document what triggered it
