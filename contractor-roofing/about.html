Our Story

Promptly. Professionally. Reliably.

Summit Roofing was founded over 15 years ago with a simple mission: deliver honest, high-quality roofing services that homeowners can trust. What started as a small, family-run operation has grown into one of the region's most respected roofing companies.

Owner and lead contractor Mike Sullivan built this company on the values he learned from his father -- show up on time, do the job right, and treat every home like your own. Those principles guide every project we take on, from a simple repair to a full roof replacement.

We believe that reliable roofing shouldn't come with surprises. That's why we provide transparent pricing, honest assessments, and clear communication from start to finish.
