Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the questions homeowners ask most about roofing projects.

Most residential roof replacements are completed in 1-2 days, depending on the size and complexity of the roof. We'll provide a specific timeline during your free estimate. Larger or multi-story homes may take 2-3 days. We always work to minimize disruption to your daily routine.
Absolutely. We have extensive experience working with all major insurance carriers. We'll document the damage, meet with your adjuster on-site, and help you navigate the entire claims process from start to finish — at no extra cost to you.
We offer a 10-year workmanship warranty on all installations in addition to the manufacturer's material warranty (25-50 years depending on the product). As a GAF-certified contractor, we can also offer their Golden Pledge warranty for maximum coverage.
Costs vary based on roof size, pitch, material choice, and complexity. Most residential roof replacements in our area range from $8,000 to $20,000. We provide free, detailed estimates with no hidden fees — and we'll never pressure you into a decision.
