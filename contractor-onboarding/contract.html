Scale National — Contractor Services Agreement

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Scale National ("Company") and the contractor identified below ("Client").

1. Services

Scale National agrees to provide the selected package of digital services as chosen during onboarding. Services may include website design and hosting, automated review request system, missed call text-back, CRM setup, lead pipeline management, and/or ads management depending on the selected package.

2. Monthly Fee & Billing

Client agrees to pay the monthly subscription fee for the selected package. Billing begins upon signing this agreement and recurs on the same date each month. Payments are processed automatically via Stripe.

3. Term & Cancellation

This agreement is month-to-month. Either party may cancel with 30 days written notice via email to eric@scalenational.com. No refunds are issued for partial months.

4. Platform Ownership

All systems, automations, workflows, and platform configurations built by Scale National remain the property of Scale National. Upon cancellation, Client retains their business data (contacts, reviews, job history) in exportable CSV format. Platform access is terminated upon cancellation.

5. Platform Buyout

Client may purchase full platform ownership and transfer at any time for a one-time fee of $1,500. This includes full account transfer, documentation, and 30 days of transition support.

6. Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to provide accurate business information, respond to onboarding requests within 5 business days, and provide necessary access (Google Business Profile, etc.) for setup.

7. Results Disclaimer

Scale National does not guarantee specific results including review counts, lead volume, or revenue increases. Results depend on factors outside our control including client responsiveness and market conditions.

8. Limitation of Liability

Scale National's total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid in the prior 30 days.

9. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Florida.
