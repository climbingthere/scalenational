Before You Hit Send

Check their last 9 posts. Find the one that overperformed. Lead with a specific observation — not a compliment.
Check Meta Ad Library. Are they running paid? If yes — what does the creative look like? If no — that's your angle.
Identify one specific gap. Weak content? No paid distribution? No UGC? Pick one — don't try to solve everything in DM 1.
Find their name in the bio, website, or tagged posts. "Hey [Name]" hits better than "Hey —".
Match the platform tone. TikTok = shorter. Instagram = one extra sentence. Texts = most stripped down.
Replace calendly.com/erickirt with your live booking link before sending.
