## Quick Personalization Checklist
*Do this before hitting send on DM 1. Takes 5 minutes. Makes a real difference.*

- **Check their last 9 posts.** What's getting the most engagement? Is there a content type that's clearly outperforming everything else? Lead with a specific observation about that — not a compliment, an actual observation.
- **Look at their current ads (if any).** Go to Meta Ad Library and search the brand name. Are they running paid? If yes, what does the creative look like — polished or raw? If no, that's your angle.
- **Identify the gap.** Do they have great product but weak content? Great content but no paid distribution? Real clients but no UGC? Pick one specific gap to address — don't try to solve everything in DM 1.
- **Note the platform you're DMing on.** TikTok DMs are more casual — shorter is better. Instagram DMs can handle one extra sentence. Cold texts need to be the most stripped-down of all. Adjust tone accordingly.
- **Use their name if you have it.** Check the bio, the website's About page, or tagged posts. "Hey [Name]" lands better than "Hey —" if you can find it without it being weird.
