Meta Ads 101

What You Need to Know
Before We Start

Meta Is Not Google

Google targets people based on what they're searching for right now. Meta targets people based on who they are — interests, behavior, age, location. You're meeting them before they know they need you.

The Learning Phase

When an ad launches, Meta's algorithm needs data — roughly 50 conversions — before it fully understands who to show your ad to. Performance in the first 1–2 weeks is normal to be unpredictable. This is expected.

The Auction System

Every ad placement is won through a live auction — scored on your bid, ad quality, and how likely someone is to interact with it. A smaller budget with a high-quality ad can outperform a big budget with a weak one.

Our Goal First

We're not trying to reach millions out of the gate. We need to find what works — the right creative, the right audience, the right offer — then scale what's winning. Testing is not optional, it's the strategy.

ROI depends on strategy, creative, targeting, your offer, and your landing page. That's exactly why we control all of it — not just the ad spend.
